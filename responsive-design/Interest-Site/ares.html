Tron: Ares follows a highly advanced program named Ares who is brought from the digital world into the real one. The story explores what happens when a sentient artificial being—designed for perfect logic and efficiency—enters human society for the first time.

As tensions rise between humans and programs, Ares becomes a bridge between the two worlds, confronting questions of identity, free will, and the consequences of technology evolving beyond human control.
