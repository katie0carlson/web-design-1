SYNOPSIS:

Brilliant programmer Kevin Flynn is pulled into the digital world of a mainframe computer. He discovers a glowing realm where programs appear as living beings and are ruled by the Master Control Program (MCP).

Trapped in this universe, Flynn teams up with a security program named Tron to overthrow the MCP and restore freedom to the system. Together, they face deadly challenges like light cycle battles and disc duels while racing to stop the MCP from taking control of both the digital and real worlds.
